About the Automonus Project


At Automonus, our goal is to teach the world how to handle its money, and how to acquire more of it.

We are a software-based company, and thus far we offer two software products:



The Academy

The academy is our tool for teaching people the basics of personal finance. The academy includes:

  • Horne's Calculator - A software program which tells you exactly how much money you have to make to afford the lifestyle of your dreams.
  • Our Course on Life and Money - A comprehensive video and curriculum based course, developed by our in-house math PhD and MENSA member, Nicholas Horne.

Check out the academy

"Auto" Analysis

The Automonus analysis tool allows you to connect your bank accounts from almost any US financial institution. From there, we break down your finances to show you how much money you earn, spend, save, and invest - as well as providing you with an individual financial plan for success.

Go to "Auto" Analysis
