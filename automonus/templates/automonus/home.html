The Automonus Project

Our goal is to teach the world how to handle its money, and how to acquire more of it.

The Automonus Project is an ongoing effort to create financial software products that help you to optimize and streamline your financial processes.



Analysis

Our Analysis system allows you to connect your bank accounts from almost any US financial institution by simply filling in your online username and password. From there, we break down your finances to show you how much money you earn, spend, save, and invest - as well as keeping track of your debts


Payments

Our payments system allows you to send money to any other user on our platform.
